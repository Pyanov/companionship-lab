Philosophy & Principles

Start with Human Need

"We're focused on building consumer products. Not about dead people, but about real people connecting with their AI friends." Every successful product I've built started with a genuine human need—grief, loneliness, self-understanding—then found the right technology to serve it.

Community Drives Innovation

"When launching a consumer app, it is crucial to test out a variety of different creative ideas for the brand and not to commit to any until receiving positive feedback." The best products emerge from deep engagement with early users.

Emotional Authenticity

AI companions succeed when they create genuine emotional value. This isn't about tricking users or replacing human connection—it's about augmenting our capacity for reflection, learning, and growth.

Ethical Innovation

"AI is a blessing and a curse. We need more scholar research, digital mental health studies to equip us for a better future." Every feature must consider its impact on user wellbeing.
